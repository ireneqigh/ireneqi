Who Am I?

Hello! I am a sophomore at the University of Pennsylvania, The Wharton School. I intend to concentrate in Marketing and Finance, and graduate with a minor in Computer Science. I am particularly interested in corporate strategy, brand management, and data analytics.

At Penn, I am a marketer for our chapter of the food publication Spoon University. I am also on the Coalition Committee of Habitat for Humanity UPenn, where I help organize our semesterly dodgeball fundraiser and fall Rake-a-Thon. In my spare time I enjoy exploring the city, yoga, painting, and food adventures.

This is a webpage mockup of a hypothetical bank's loan application process. My goals were to make the process seem easy to understand and to facilitate the process of choosing the right loan.

I chose to include a timeline of the application process to provide a quick and simple overview, and organized the packages in distinct blocks. If a customer has unanswered questions, a button at the bottom of the page would lead to a chat screen, and the customer would be connected to an employee.
